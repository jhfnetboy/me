About Me

Huifeng (Jason) Jiao is a PhD candidate in Digital Innovation at Chiang Mai University [1, 2], with a research focus on blockchain accounts and security, aiming to empower individuals through technology [3]. His doctoral research explores areas such as Gas payment optimization, self-custodial accounts, and social recovery under Ethereum account abstraction [4, 5].

Driven by a passion for individual freedom and the potential of decentralized technologies [6], Jason is also an active open source contributor [7] and a Ethereum builder, currently he is reading his PhD and served as Cofounder/Tech lead at Soul Wallet [7, 8]. He is the OG of a Web3 tech community (DApp Learning community) [9] and has been involved in the Ethereum community, participating in Devcons and hackathons [10].

Prior to his current endeavors, Jason held significant technical leadership roles, including M3/T8 expert at JD.COM and P9 Tech expert at SchoolPal, where he managed large teams and oversaw multi-BU operations [7]. He also has experience in building an online e-commerce platform with substantial GMV and refactoring SAAS systems [9].
