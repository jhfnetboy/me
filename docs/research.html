Research Interests

As a researcher, Huifeng's primary focus is on how to enable ordinary individuals to gain freedom by acquiring their own time, space, and choice [6]. He posits the theory of "HyperCapital," aiming to establish individual ownership in value creation and distribution within a free market framework, potentially facilitated by blockchain technology [6].

Key Research Areas Include:

  • Blockchain Account and Security [3]
  • Blockchain Basic Decentralized Computing [3]
  • Artificial Intelligence in Individual Decision Making on Blockchain [3]
  • Complex System and Community Sustainability [3]
  • Social Capital and Individual Value Quantification [3, 11]
  • Web3 and Decentralized Application [3]

PhD Research

Huifeng's PhD research focuses on Blockchain Account [3]. He has completed a conference paper titled: "Blockchain Account: Credential Control Balance" [3].

Ongoing Research Sub-topics:

  • Gas payment optimization under Ethereum Account Abstraction: Exploring SuperPaymaster and related research to improve the cost, efficiency, and UX of gas payments [4].
  • Self-custodial accounts under Ethereum Account Abstraction: Researching AirAccount and related concepts to address the security, privacy, and UX of self-custodial accounts, considering the implications of ERC4337 and EIP7702 [4].
  • Social Recovery under Ethereum Account Abstraction: Investigating SocialRecovery solutions to enhance the security, privacy, and UX of account recovery in decentralized systems [5].
  • Further Research Topics include contract account secondary verification, TEE applications in community nodes, SessionKey usage, anonymous addresses (Stealth Address), DVT and BLS, blockchain accounts for feature phones in Africa, blockchain account value in on-chain games, and the UX considerations of account/wallet/chain abstraction [5].
  • Research related to the HyperCapital theory's account research and practice [5].
